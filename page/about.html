I was born in East Berlin and raised in Sussex, experiencing my early years within the confines of the iron curtain.

Currently residing in Brooklyn and frequently traveling, I aim to capture the beauty, elegance, resilience, and passion of individuals through my portraiture.

Lately, I have been focusing on photographing environmentalists, adventurers, scientists, and those dedicated to advancing humanity.

In my photography, I place people in exaggerated scenarios that emphasize a particular facet of their personality, seeking to convey a sense of determination and strength.
